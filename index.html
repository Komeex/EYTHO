Essay Poster Competition

The Essay Competition offers participants the chance to express their critical thoughts on current issues in Bali’s hospitality industry. Focusing on challenges and opportunities within the sector, participants are encouraged to propose innovative solutions to help Bali maintain its appeal as a global destination. This competition helps participants improve their writing, critical thinking, and ability to present ideas clearly. Winners will be judged based on originality, depth of analysis, and writing quality. Join us and contribute your voice to shaping Bali’s tourism future!
